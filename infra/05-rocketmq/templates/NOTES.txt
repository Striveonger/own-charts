RocketMQ has been deployed successfully.

To connect to RocketMQ:

1. Check the pod status:
   kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/name=rocketmq

2. Get the NameServer address:
   kubectl get svc {{ include "rocketmq.fullname" . }}-ns -n infra -o jsonpath='{.spec.clusterIP}'

3. Get the Broker address:
   kubectl get svc {{ include "rocketmq.fullname" . }}-broker -n infra -o jsonpath='{.spec.clusterIP}'

NameServer: {{ include "rocketmq.fullname" . }}-ns:{{ .Values.nameserver.port }}
Broker: {{ include "rocketmq.fullname" . }}-broker:{{ .Values.broker.port }}

To access the RocketMQ dashboard (if installed):
   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "rocketmq.fullname" . }}-ns {{ .Values.nameserver.port }}:{{ .Values.nameserver.port }} -n infra
